## The webAI Platform
--- 
### Navigator


  Design, build, and customize AI applications in a visual workflow builder. Drag and drop elements to create everything from chatbots to document processing systems.

- Visual workflow designer
- Pre-built templates 
- Custom LLM training

### Companion

Bring your Navigator-built applications to life as a desktop chat interface. Deploy your custom models and interact with them privately on your device.

- Desktop AI chat application
- Connect to Navigator-deployed models
- Private, local conversations
- Multi-model support
