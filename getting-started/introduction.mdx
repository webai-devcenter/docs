## Core Concepts
Understanding these concepts will help you get the most out of webAI:

### Local-First Architecture
Unlike cloud AI services, webAI runs entirely on your devices.

- No internet required for AI conversations (after initial setup)
- Complete data privacy - nothing leaves your environment
- Predictable performance - no network latency or service outages
- Full control over when and how your AI runs

### Visual Workflow Building
Navigator uses a drag-and-drop interface to build AI applications.

- Elements: Individual components like AI models, data processors, APIs
- Connections: Lines that show how data flows between elements
- Templates: Pre-built workflows you can customize
- Deployments: Running applications that users can interact with

### Clusters and Deployments
Your AI applications run on clusters of devices:

- Clusters: Groups of devices that can run your AI models
- Deployments: Running instances of your workflows
- Scaling: Add more devices to handle more users or larger models
- Management: Monitor and control your AI applications
